Privacy Policy

Last updated: 29/04/26

This privacy policy explains how [Your Service Name] ("we", "us", "the service") collects, uses, and shares contact information about online businesses. It is written for the people whose business contacts may appear in our database, the customers who subscribe to our service, and anyone else who wants to understand how we operate.

If you have any questions about this policy or want to remove your business contact information from our database, contact us at JBH.Signals@gmail.com.

Who runs this service

This service is operated by an individual based in Northern Ireland, United Kingdom. For all data protection matters, contact JBH.Signals.

What data we collect

We collect publicly listed business contact information from online stores hosted on the Shopify platform. Specifically, for each store we may collect:

  • Store name and website URL
  • Public business email addresses (e.g. info@, sales@, hello@, contact@)
  • Public business phone numbers
  • Public links to the store's social media accounts (Instagram, Facebook, TikTok, X/Twitter, YouTube, Pinterest)
  • Product types sold and approximate product count
  • The country in which the store appears to be operating

We only collect information that is publicly displayed on a store's own website — typically on contact, about, or footer pages. We do not collect customer data, order data, or any information that requires login or payment to access.

Where the data comes from

All data is sourced directly from the public-facing pages of Shopify storefronts. We discover stores via public search engines and confirm them as Shopify stores via Shopify's own publicly accessible product feeds. We do not buy data from third-party brokers, do not use leaked databases, and do not collect data from social media profiles beyond the public links a business chooses to display on its own website.

Why we collect it (lawful basis)

For UK and EU recipients, our lawful basis under UK GDPR and EU GDPR Article 6(1)(f) is legitimate interest: the operation of a business-to-business prospecting service whose subscribers are professional sales and marketing teams looking to contact other businesses. We have considered the balance between this interest and the privacy interests of the businesses whose contacts appear in our database, and we believe the balance is appropriate because:

  • Only public business contacts are collected
  • Only role-based and business-display contacts are prioritised
  • Anyone listed can be removed on request, free of charge, within 72 hours
  • This privacy policy is published openly so anyone can understand how we operate

For US recipients, this activity is governed primarily by the CAN-SPAM Act, which applies to recipients of commercial email and is the responsibility of the senders (our customers).

How we use the data

The data is provided to subscribing customers as a weekly business prospect list, delivered via a private Google Sheet shared only with the named subscriber. We require all subscribers to agree to:

  • Use the data solely for legitimate business-to-business outreach
  • Comply with all applicable laws in their own jurisdiction (CAN-SPAM, PECR, ePrivacy, GDPR, CCPA, etc.)
  • Honour opt-out requests from recipients immediately
  • Not resell, redistribute, or repurpose the data for any other use

Who we share it with

We share the data only with the specific paying subscriber it was prepared for. We do not publish the data, do not list businesses on a public website, and do not share with any other third parties beyond the subscriber and the technical providers we use to operate the service (Google Sheets for delivery, Gmail for email digests, GitHub Actions for processing — all standard infrastructure providers operating under their own privacy commitments).

How long we keep it

Data is retained in our database for as long as the service operates, in order to avoid re-contacting businesses that have previously been delivered to a subscriber. Opt-out entries are retained indefinitely so that opted-out businesses are never re-added.
